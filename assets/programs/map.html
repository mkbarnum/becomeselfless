MISSIONARY ASSISTANCE PROGRAM

PURPOSE

The purpose of the SELFLESS Missionary Assistance Program is to provide an opportunity for individuals – those who otherwise may not have to opportunity due to financial constraints – to increase their faith as well as gain leadership, management, and other valuable skills through serving a full-time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. The amount awarded will include the monthly cost of missionary service as determined by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints as well as other necessary expenses incurred in preparation for missionary service such as: clothing and supplies.
