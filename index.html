About Us

SELFLESS BEGINS SELF

We believe that the way to build stronger families and communities starts with individuals. Our mission is to empower people with the tools and resources to become self-sufficient. SELFLESS aims to offer these resources through our various financial need based programs. We focus on education and self improvement as the keys to self-sufficiency. SELFLESS really does begin with "self".

Our Core
Values.

Integrity is the foundation of our work. We operate with honesty and transparency in every action.

Service to others is our highest calling. We dedicate our time and energy to lifting those in need.

Compassion drives us to understand and alleviate the struggles of our community members.

Faith in God and in the potential of every individual fuels our mission and perseverance.

Education is the key to unlocking potential and breaking the cycle of poverty permanently.

Self-Reliance empowers individuals to provide for themselves and their families with dignity.

Community strength is built when neighbors help neighbors grow and succeed together.

Hope is what we build. A belief that tomorrow can be better than today through effort.
